A High-Performance Engine for Your Independent Practice

Transitioning from a high-burnout corporate system to an independent practice shouldn't mean facing the "Small Business Barrier" alone. Controlled-Health provides the operational and analytical infrastructure to make your transition seamless so you can focus 100% of your time on medicine.

Dynamic Panel Equilibrium

Our algorithm calculates your specific "Optimal Patient Number" for the lifestyle that you want to live. We factor in regional economic data and your desired workload to find the precise equilibrium point where revenue is maximized. Our algorithm will also assist us in predicting how long it will take to reach your desired patient panel size for your area.

Dedicated Account Management

We act as your outsourced business office. Our account managers handle regional marketing and patient discovery to quickly build and maintain your ideal panel size. This is your point of contact with our company allowing you to be heard so that we are developing the best product for our Physicians.

Subscription Based Model

Leave erratic Fee-for-Service income behind. We help you structure tiered pricing that rewards long-term patient commitments, securing stable and predictable subscription revenue. This eliminates your battles for insurance so that you will no longer be checking the boxes for payments, but rather focusing patient visits on providing higher quality services improving health outcomes.
