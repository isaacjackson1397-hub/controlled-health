The Problem

The current healthcare system prioritizes volume over value, leading to unprecedented physician burnout and administrative bloat. For physicians, this means working in a chaotic and rushed environment, missing the oppourtunity to learn enough about a patient's life to make a real impact on improving the long-term health of their patients. For patients, this means long, expensive waits for short visits with the physician. In modern healthcare, administration is one culprit for increasing expenses. Today, there is roughly one physician for every ten administrators, and we want to change that.

Our Solution

Controlled-Health provides the missing link for physicians ready to transition to a sustainable, independent, subscription-based practice, and for patients wanting to improve their healthcare outcomes through higher-quality care. We are the connection between physicians and patients, removing the insurance middleman, driving costs down, and elevating the quality of care.

Our Mission

Our goal is to revolutionize the way that healthcare is provided and received, beginning in the United States. Starting with direct primary care, we will emphasize enabling better patient-physician relationships, reducing healthcare costs, and providing a low-risk transition for physicians currently in large corporate systems. By realigning financial incentives, our services will empower physicians and patients to reclaim their healthcare relationship through a transparent, subscription-based marketplace.
