About Us

We are a team dedicated to realigning healthcare incentives and building the infrastructure for independent medicine. Our team is focused on creativity, curiosity, and execution.

Isaac Jackson

Co-Founder & CEO

Isaac is a junior pursuing a B.S. in Economics at Weber State University, with minors in Math and Health Administrative Services. His professional background includes experience in Procurement and Enterprise Hosting (FinOps), complemented by technical proficiency in database design, SQL, Python, R, and Stata. The vision for Controlled-Health was created through Isaac's undergraduate research and proximity to an independent DPC physician.
